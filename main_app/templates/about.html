About Us

VISION, MANDATE & OUR STORY

VISION

Biba has a long term vision of building a world class investment management firm (machine). For this goal, Biba aims to build a multistrategy machinery that will contain VC business, PE business, HF business, REITs, and Alternative Asset investment business.

MANDATE

Given our lofty ambitions as a firm, we will craft a Roadmap of short term goals,mid-term goals, and long term goals, which will be a year, three years, and ten years respectively. Primarily we want to build a VC business.

OUR STORY

Biba our name comes from the word kubiba, a kinyarwanda word that means to flower or to plant a seed, which is our goal as a fund.
